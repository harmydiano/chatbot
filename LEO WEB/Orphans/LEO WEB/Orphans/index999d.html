About us

Leo Finance is a financial platform currently being developed to tackle one of the biggest problems in the crypto community which is enabling cryptocurrency owners to gain instant access to their crypto wealth without liquidating or selling off their valued crypto assets. Hence promoting the popular crypto ‘HODL’ culture that ensures crypto owners maximize the full profit on their cryptocurrency investment while taking advantage of our crypto-backed fiat loans.

How it works?

To procure crypto-backed loans on the Leo platform, crypto owners have undergo some simple steps

Firstly, crypto owners have to transfer their crypto assets to leo wallet. Afterwards, their loan limit is automatically calculated and is then avaliable to them via same/next day bank transfers or through the leo credit card.

Our increament options are flexible which includes payment by crypto assets, bank wire or through our Leo token which comes with a discount.

Clients can withdraw their crypto assets from their leo wallet as long as they do not have an outstanding balance to complete their loan repayment.
